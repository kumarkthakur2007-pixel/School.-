Our Legacy

Seven decades of nurturing scholars in Mithila.

1955
Foundation — Mithila High English School
Established to provide quality education to children of Mithila region.
1970s
Expansion to Higher Secondary
+2 level introduced. Science and Arts streams opened.
1980s
Government Recognition
Recognised under BSEB Patna — a new era of growth and stability.
2000s
Co-Education Initiative
Became fully co-educational, welcoming boys and girls in Classes 9–12.
